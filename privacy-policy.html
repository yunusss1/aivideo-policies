Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 20, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to AIVIDO ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our AI video generation mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do NOT collect, store, or process any personal information. AIVIDO is designed with privacy in mind.

Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication for user accounts. Firebase may collect minimal authentication data (such as user ID) necessary for account functionality. This data is handled by Google Firebase and subject to their privacy policy.

Usage Data

We do NOT track your usage patterns, browsing history, or any behavioral data.

Crash Reports

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports to improve app stability. These reports contain technical information about app crashes but do NOT include any personal or identifiable information.

Data Storage

Your credit balance and video generation history are stored securely in Firebase Firestore, associated only with your anonymous user ID. We do not have access to any personal information linked to this data.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and crash reporting
  • Google Play Billing: For in-app purchases

These services have their own privacy policies governing their data collection and use.

In-App Purchases

All payment processing is handled by Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Your purchase history is managed by Google Play.

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your data with any third parties. The only data sharing occurs through the third-party services mentioned above, which is necessary for app functionality.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your account and associated data at any time
  • Request information about data we store (which is minimal)
  • Opt out of crash reporting by not using the app

Changes to This Policy
