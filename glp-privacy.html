Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2026

GLP Monitor ("we", "our", "the app") is a wellness tracking application designed to help users monitor their GLP-1 medication journey. We take your privacy seriously.

Summary: Your health data stays on your device. We do not sell, share, or collect your personal health information.

1. Data We Collect

Data stored locally on your device:

  • Medication type, dose, and injection schedule
  • Weight logs and body measurements
  • Side effect logs
  • Mood and hunger logs
  • Injection site rotation history
  • User preferences (units, goals, notification settings)

All of this data is stored exclusively in your device's local storage using Apple's Core Data framework. It is never uploaded to our servers.

Data processed by third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: If you subscribe to GLP Monitor PRO, RevenueCat processes your purchase through Apple's App Store. We receive your subscription status (active/inactive) but never your payment details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • AI Insights (optional, PRO only): If you use the AI Weekly Insight feature, an anonymized summary of your recent health trends (no personal identifiers) is sent to our secure backend to generate personalized insights. This data is not stored on our servers after processing.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your location or IP address
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Any data for analytics or advertising

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All health and wellness data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. If you delete the app, all data is permanently removed. We do not offer cloud backup at this time.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — for app distribution and in-app purchases
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management
  • Fal AI (OpenRouter) — for AI-generated health insights (PRO feature, optional)

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Notifications

The app may send local notifications (injection reminders, weigh-in reminders, side effect check-ins) if you enable them. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not involve any server communication.

6. Children's Privacy

GLP Monitor is not intended for use by children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from Settings → Delete All Logs or Settings → Reset Everything. Uninstalling the app also permanently removes all stored data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
